**Design Movement**: Afro-Modern Tech Minimalism
**Core Principles**:
1. **Trust & Transparency**: Clean lines, open spaces, and clear hierarchy to build confidence.
2. **Accessibility**: High contrast, large touch targets, and legible typography for diverse users.
3. **Local Authenticity**: Integrating Nigerian patterns and motifs subtly into a modern tech interface.
4. **Mobile-First Utility**: Designing for thumb-friendly interactions and fast scanning.

**Color Philosophy**:
- **Primary**: Nigerian Green (#008751) - Represents growth, agriculture, and national pride. Used for primary actions and branding.
- **Secondary**: Crisp White (#FFFFFF) - For cleanliness and clarity.
- **Accent**: Deep Charcoal (#333333) for text and high contrast elements.
- **Warmth**: A touch of Terracotta or Gold for warmth and human connection, avoiding cold corporate feel.

**Layout Paradigm**:
- **Asymmetric Balance**: Moving away from strict center alignment to dynamic, asymmetric layouts that guide the eye.
- **Card-Based Architecture**: Using distinct cards for services and profiles to organize information clearly.
- **Fluid Grids**: Responsive layouts that adapt seamlessly from mobile to desktop.

**Signature Elements**:
- **"The Bridge" Motif**: Subtle curved lines or connecting elements representing the bridge between users and providers.
- **Patterned Backgrounds**: Very subtle, low-opacity geometric patterns inspired by Ankara fabrics in section backgrounds.
- **Circular Imagery**: Using circular frames for people (providers/users) to emphasize community and connection.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- **Direct & Reassuring**: Buttons provide immediate feedback. Hover states are clear.
- **Smooth Transitions**: Sections slide in gently to feel polished but not distracting.

**Animation**:
- **Entrance**: Elements fade up and in as user scrolls.
- **Hover**: Buttons lift slightly with shadow increase.
- **Counter**: The waitlist number counts up on load.

**Typography System**:
- **Headings**: **Clash Display** or **Syne** - Bold, modern, with character.
- **Body**: **Plus Jakarta Sans** or **Inter** - Highly readable, clean, and professional.
</text>
